# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is ANIA Player?
ANIA Player is a frame-based avatar playback system that brings AI-generated avatars to life with intelligent lip-sync and idle animations.

### Is ANIA Player free?
Yes, ANIA Player is completely free to download and use for both personal and commercial purposes.

### Do I need to pay for avatars?
ANIA Player is free. Avatars (.ania files) can be free or premium, available on the ANIA Models Market.

### Can I create my own avatars?
Yes! Use ANIA Creators to design and submit your own avatars. All submissions require admin approval.

## Technical

### What are the system requirements?
- **OS:** Windows 10/11, Linux (kernel 5.4+)
- **RAM:** 512MB minimum
- **CPU:** Dual-core 2.0GHz+
- **Audio:** System-recognized microphone

### Which platforms are supported?
- Windows 10/11 (x64)
- Linux x86_64
- Linux ARM64

### How much CPU/RAM does it use?
Very little! Resource usage depends on avatar complexity. Use hotkeys to mute/hide and reduce usage even further.

### Does it work with OBS/streaming software?
Yes! The chroma key support makes it perfect for OBS and other streaming software.

## Avatars & .ania Files

### Where do I get .ania files?
Download them from the [ANIA Models Market](https://aniamodels.shop). Free and premium options available.

### Can I use any .ania file?
Only validated .ania files approved by ANIA administrators will work. This ensures quality and copyright compliance.

### How do I open .ania files?
Just double-click. ANIA Player automatically associates with .ania files during installation.

### Can I share .ania files?
Yes, if you created them or have permission. All .ania files from the market include usage terms.

## Features

### Does it support microphone input?
Yes! Configure any system audio interface in settings for real-time speech detection.

### Can I customize the avatar size and speed?
Absolutely. Adjust size, frame speed, startup behavior, and more in settings.

### What are the hotkeys?
Hotkeys allow quick mute/hide controls. Check settings for the full list and customization options.

### Does it have chroma key?
Yes! Built-in chroma key support for transparent backgrounds.

## Troubleshooting

### The avatar won't load
- Ensure the .ania file is valid and approved
- Check your internet connection (validation requires server access)
- Try re-downloading the .ania file

### High CPU/RAM usage
- Use hotkeys to mute/hide when not in use
- Lower frame rate in settings
- Choose simpler avatar models

### Microphone not detected
- Check system audio settings
- Ensure microphone permissions are granted
- Restart ANIA Player after connecting microphone

### Avatar appears with black background
- Enable chroma key in settings
- Ensure your streaming software is configured for chroma key
